FAQ

Common questions

Who is Sequora built for?

Primarily students in Bangladesh sitting Edexcel IGCSE, Cambridge IGCSE, Edexcel A-Level / AS-Level, Cambridge A-Level, O Level, and ACCA. The free tier and bKash payment are designed to be accessible locally — no international cards or foreign-currency pricing.

How does bKash payment work?

Send the subscription amount to the bKash number shown in-app, then submit your Transaction ID (TrxID) in the Toolkit section. We verify manually within 24 hours and activate your plan. No recurring charges — renewals are manual and at your discretion.

Where do the syllabus topics come from?

Topics are extracted from official, publicly available syllabus specification PDFs published by Edexcel (Pearson) and Cambridge Assessment. For boards or subjects without a built-in template (including MBBS and custom curricula), you can upload your own PDF and our AI extracts the full topic list automatically.

Is my study data private?

Yes. All data is stored against your user account with row-level security — only you can access your topics, sessions, and progress. PDFs uploaded for AI extraction are processed in-memory and never stored on our servers. You can export or delete all your data at any time from the Toolkit tab.

What AI model powers the advisor?

The AI advisor is powered by Google Gemini 2.5 Flash. You generate a structured summary of your current study position and the AI gives you specific, honest guidance on what to prioritise with the days you have left before your exam.

Can I cancel or get a refund?

Plans are monthly or 6-monthly with no automatic renewal — if you don't renew, your plan simply expires. For refund requests within 48 hours of payment activation, email officialfindrumtechnologies@gmail.com and we'll handle it case by case.
