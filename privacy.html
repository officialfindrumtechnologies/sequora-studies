SEQUORA STUDIES

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 9 July 2026
Draft notice: This document was drafted to get Sequora Studies to launch quickly. It has not been reviewed by a lawyer. Given the service handles data from students who may be minors, get it reviewed by a qualified lawyer in Bangladesh before treating it as fully compliant.

1. What this covers

This policy explains what data Sequora Studies collects, why, how it's stored, and what control you have over it. "We," "us," and "Sequora" refer to Sequora Studies, operated by Findrum Technologies.

2. Information we collect

CategoryExamples
Account infoEmail address, display name, password (hashed, we never see it in plain text)
Study dataSubjects, topics, study sessions, exam papers, logged mistakes, notes, exam date, qualification/exam board
Payment infobKash transaction ID, amount, and the bKash phone number you optionally provide for verification. We never collect your bKash PIN or full account details — payment happens directly between you and bKash outside our systems.
Usage dataLogin timestamps, feature usage (e.g. AI Advisor call counts, for quota enforcement)
Optional AI inputsText or documents you submit to AI features (syllabus PDFs, study prompts) — sent to Google's Gemini API to generate responses

3. How we use your information

  • To provide and operate the study tracker (dashboards, recall scheduling, progress tracking)
  • To verify and activate paid subscriptions
  • To send transactional emails (signup verification, password reset, activation confirmation, optional weekly progress reports)
  • To power AI features you choose to use (study advice, question generation, source search)
  • To maintain security and prevent abuse

We do not sell your data, and we do not use your study data for advertising.

4. Third-party services we use

We rely on established third-party infrastructure providers to run Sequora, each of which processes some of your data strictly on our behalf and under their own security and privacy practices:

FunctionPurpose
Database & authenticationStoring your account and study data, and verifying your sign-ins
Application hostingServing the Sequora web app
Transactional emailSending signup verification, password reset, and activation emails
AI processingGenerating study advice, questions, and citation suggestions — only when you use those features

We don't publish the specific vendors we use for infrastructure. If you have a legal or security reason to need this information (e.g. a data protection request), contact us at the email below.

5. Data storage and security

Your data is stored in a secure database with access controls scoped per user, meaning only you (and, where necessary, Sequora administrators for support and payment verification) can access it. Traffic to Sequora is encrypted in transit (HTTPS).

6. Data retention

We keep your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all associated data (subjects, topics, sessions, papers, errors, subscription history) is permanently deleted, generally within a few minutes.

7. Your rights

You can, at any time, from the app's Toolkit tab:

  • Export a full copy of your data as JSON
  • Permanently delete your account and all associated data

You can also update or correct your profile information (name, qualification, exam date) at any time via Edit Profile.

8. Children's privacy

Sequora is intended for students aged 13 and up, many of whom are minors studying for school exams. If you are under 18, we assume a parent or guardian is aware of your use of the service. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has created an account, contact us and we'll remove it.

9. Cookies and local storage

We use browser local storage to keep you signed in and to cache your data for a faster experience. We don't use third-party advertising or tracking cookies.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the service evolves. We'll post the updated version here with a new "Last updated" date.
